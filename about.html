Matthew Aaron Burr

Born and raised in Central Arkansas. Graduate of Hendrix College (B.A., 2008) and the University of Arkansas School of Law in Fayetteville (J.D.). Studied international criminal law in St. Petersburg, Russia, conservation and field ecology in Costa Rica, and aviation at Central Flying Service in Little Rock, Arkansas.

Maintains a broad general practice with a focus on criminal defense. Known for careful, detail-oriented work in complex legal and factual matters, and for providing thorough, strategic representation across a wide range of issues. Approaches matters with conviction where injustice is present, guided by a commitment to doing what is right and addressing wrongful conduct.

Approaches each matter with technical precision, sound judgment, and a commitment to honesty, integrity, and fair access to justice.
